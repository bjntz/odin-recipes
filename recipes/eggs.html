Deviled Eggs

Description

Hardboiled, deviled eggs flavored with garlic and mustard.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Mayonnaise
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic powder
  • Ground mustard
  • Paprika

Steps

  1. Boil eggs about 12 minutes or until hardboiled.
  2. Cool eggs, remove shells.
  3. Cut eggs in half. Separate yolks from whites of eggs, place into mixing bowl.
  4. Smash yolks with fork until resembling coarse crumbs.
  5. Add mayonnaise and mix until creamy.
  6. Add salt, pepper, garlic powder, and ground mustard to taste.
  7. Spoon yolk mixture back into empty egg halves.
  8. Sprinkle with paprika and enjoy.
