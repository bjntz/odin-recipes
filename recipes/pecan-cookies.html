Chocolate Pecan Cookies

Description

Chewy chocolate cookies with pecan pieces for an added crunch.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup salted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 oz unsweetened baker's chocolate, melted
  • 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup pecans

Steps

  1. Combine butter, salt and vanilla and mix well.
  2. Add sugar and mix until creamy.
  3. Add eggs, mix.
  4. Add melted chocolate, mix.
  5. Fold in nuts.
  6. Bake on greased cookie sheet at 325 degreees for 10-12 minutes.
